Uses of Arduino

Arduino empowers students to unleash their creativity, develop problem-solving skills, and build innovative projects. Its adaptability makes it an ideal tool for educators to inspire the next generation of inventors and engineers, fostering a deep appreciation for STEM fields.

Introducing: Smart Plant

The "Smart Plant: An Arduino Based Automatic Irrigation System" represents a highly cost-effective, portable, compact, and remarkably low-maintenance solution engineered to tackle the pervasive issue of plant watering. This innovative system excels at meticulously dispensing the precise amount of water required for potted plants, thus acting as a reliable shield against their untimely demise due to dehydration.
